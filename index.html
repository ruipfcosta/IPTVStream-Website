Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is IPTV Stream?

    IPTV Stream is an Apple TV app which allows you to manage your M3U playlist and watch your favourite channels in an easy and intuitive way.

  • How do I use IPTV Stream?

    Simply download the app on your Apple TV, add your M3U playlists and you're ready to go!

  • How much does it cost?

    IPTV Stream is free and always will be, however we believe in continuous improvement and innovation. To support the further development and maintenance of the app, we have included optional in-app purchases that offer additional features.

  • Does IPTV Stream include any content or playlists?

    No, no content or playlists are included along with the app, so you'll need to add your own M3U playlists. There are plenty of publicly available channels and playlists.
